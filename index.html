🌟 Features

⚔️ Catch Characters!

Explore channels to encounter dynamic character spawns — be the fastest to react and claim them! Each catch adds a new hero to your party, from familiar faces to rare legends.

✨ Wish Characters!

Spend your Primogems and test your luck with a stylish wishing system. Unlock exclusive characters and powerful gear through visually satisfying gacha-style pulls.

🏆 Compete with Others!

Challenge friends and foes in PvP duels, climb seasonal leaderboards, and earn glory, mora, and rewards as you rise to the top of the adventurer rankings.

📖 About PokeImpact

PokeImpact is more than just a Discord bot — it's a beautifully blended universe of iconic gaming inspirations. Drawing from the elemental mastery of Genshin Impact, the strategic village progression of Clash of Clans, the beloved collection system from Pokétwo by Oliver, the magical combat of Cookie Run Kingdom, and the classic journey of Pokémon, PokeImpact brings a fantasy-rich adventure to your Discord server. Summon legendary heroes, climb through battle floors, and immerse yourself in quests — all wrapped in a sleek, animated Genshin-like aesthetic.

📍 Roadmap

Our development journey fuses the best elements from iconic games into one immersive Discord bot experience:

🏆 Trophy System (Clash of Clans)

Climb competitive ladders by battling others. Win matches, earn trophies, and unlock seasonal rewards. Top players will be immortalized on leaderboards!

🔼 Level & Rank Progression (Cookie Run Kingdom)

Every action earns you EXP — from catching characters to completing quests. Level up to unlock new ranks, exclusive features, and cosmetic titles.

🎯 Character Catching (Pokémon & Pokétwo)

Experience fast-paced drops in channels! Compete to catch iconic characters before anyone else. Expand your roster and chase after ultra-rares!

💫 Genshin-Inspired World

From Elemental affinities and Wish banners to questing systems and animated UI — everything is styled to reflect the beautiful aesthetic and depth of Genshin Impact.
